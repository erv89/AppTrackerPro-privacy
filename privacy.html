Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Thank you for using Finance Tracker Pro ("the App"). This privacy policy explains how we handle your personal financial data.

1. Data Storage and Ownership

We believe your financial data belongs to you. All data is stored locally on your device.

We do not operate a backend server, and we do not view, sell, or store your transactions, budget categories, or savings goals. Your data stays on your iPhone unless you explicitly choose to back it up or export it.

2. Information We Collect

Since the App functions locally, we collect minimal data required for app functionality:

  • Financial Data: Transactions, amounts, and categories are stored in a local database on your device.
  • Images: Receipt photos are stored in your device's file system.
  • Biometrics: We use Apple's FaceID/TouchID for app security. We simply receive a "Pass/Fail" confirmation from the iOS system; we never access your biometric data directly.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for specific app features:

  • Apple iCloud: If you enable iCloud Backup in your device settings, your data may be encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud container. We do not have access to this.
  • Apple StoreKit: We use Apple's payment system for subscriptions (Pro Access). Apple processes these payments; we do not store your credit card information.

4. Data Backups

The App provides tools for you to generate backups (JSON/CSV). These files are generated locally. If you choose to share these files (e.g., via Email or AirDrop), that action is handled by your device's standard sharing menu.
